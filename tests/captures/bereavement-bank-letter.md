If it would assist, I am happy to provide additional identifying information such as his date of birth or last known address upon request.

Could you please let me know what documentation you require in order to proceed? I would also be grateful if you could confirm the details of any accounts, current balances, and any outstanding obligations or direct debits associated with his name.

I would also ask that ANZ Bank New Zealand remove my father from any marketing lists and suspend all automated correspondence addressed to him with immediate effect.

Please do not hesitate to contact me using the details below. I would welcome any guidance you can offer on the process and appreciate your care in handling this matter.
