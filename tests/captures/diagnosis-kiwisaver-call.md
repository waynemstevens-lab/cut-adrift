# Diagnosis — KiwiSaver / provider insurance call script

Panel: `diagnosis-kiwisaver-call`  ·  Country: NZ

---

What to say:

- "Hi, my name is [your full name] and my date of birth is [your date of birth]. I hold a KiwiSaver account with ANZ and I'd like to check what insurance cover, if any, is attached to my membership."
- "I don't need to go into any personal health details right now — I just want to understand what cover I might have and how it works."
- "Is there someone in the insurance or member services team who can help me with that?"


What to ask:

- "Do I have any insurance cover linked to my ANZ KiwiSaver account — things like life cover, total and permanent disability, trauma or critical illness cover, or income protection?"
- "If I do have cover, what is the sum insured for each type?"
- "What are the conditions for making a claim — and does any of the cover pay out on diagnosis of a serious illness, rather than only if I can no longer work?"
- "Is the cover tied to me making active contributions? Would it be affected if my contributions paused or stopped?"
- "Can you send me the full policy wording and a summary of my cover in writing — by email or post — so I have it to refer to?"


What to listen for:

- Whether any trauma or critical illness cover pays out on diagnosis itself, rather than only after a waiting period or once you are unable to work. That distinction matters a lot.
- Whether your cover lapses or is affected if contributions stop — important to know before making any changes to your job or contribution settings.
- Any exclusions, stand-down periods, or pre-existing condition clauses that might apply.
- Whether they are describing group cover arranged through ANZ, or a separate individual policy — the claim process can differ.
- Do not make any changes to your contributions or membership until you have the policy wording in front of you and have had a chance to read it carefully.
