# Incapacity — questions for the medical team (fresh detailed run)

Panel: `incapacity-gp-questions`  ·  Country: NZ

---

What is happening and what to expect

- Can you explain in plain terms where Dad is at with his dementia right now, and what stage or level of functioning that represents?
- Given that he is leaving the stove on and getting lost, how significant are these as signs of progression?
- What are you expecting to change over the coming weeks and months, and at what point would things become more urgent?
- Can you write down the key points from today for me to take away?


Safety at home and driving

- In your view, is it still safe for Dad to be living alone, and what is that based on?
- If it is not safe, or becoming unsafe, what are the next steps we should be taking now?
- Is Dad still safe to drive, and what is the process for having that formally assessed or reviewed?
- Is there anything you are required to report to the New Zealand Transport Agency, and what does that mean for him?


Support, assessments, and funding

- What assessments can Dad have to properly evaluate what support he needs, and who arranges those?
- Is he eligible for a needs assessment through a Needs Assessment and Service Coordination service, and how do we get that started?
- What home support services are funded and available to him, and who do we contact to apply?
- Are there any other funded services, equipment, or respite options we should know about?
- Is there anything I as a family carer am entitled to in terms of support or recognition?


Medications and warning signs

- Is his current medication still appropriate given how things have progressed?
- What side effects or changes should I be watching for, and what should I do if I notice them?
- What signs would tell me that things have reached a point where I need to act urgently, and who do I call?


Follow-up and contacts

- Who is Dad's main point of contact for his dementia care going forward, and is that you or a specialist?
- Should he be referred to a memory service or geriatrician, and how does that work?
- How do I reach you or the practice if something comes up before the next appointment?
- When should we come back, and what will you want to review at that point?
