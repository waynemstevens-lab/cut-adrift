# Incapacity — questions for the medical team

Panel: `incapacity-gp-questions`  ·  Country: NZ

---

What is happening now and what to expect

- Can you explain in plain language where Mum is in her recovery right now?
- What is the likely timeframe for discharge, and what needs to happen before that decision is made?
- Can you give me a written summary of her current needs and the plan so far?
- Who makes the final call on whether she is ready to go home?


Whether home is safe or rest-home care is needed

- Based on her current level of need, is going home a realistic option, or does she need rest-home level care?
- What assessment has been done, or still needs to be done, to answer that question?
- If she does need rest-home care, how does that process work and how long does it usually take to arrange in this area?
- If she goes home and it turns out not to be safe, what happens next and who do I call?


What support is funded and how to access it

- What support is she entitled to through ACC, the DHB, or the Ministry of Health, given that her needs follow a stroke?
- Is a needs assessment through a Needs Assessment and Service Coordination service required, and has that been arranged?
- What is actually funded versus what we would need to pay for privately?
- Who organises the funded services, and do I need to do anything to get them started?


Day-to-day care and what she will need

- What help will she need with daily tasks like washing, dressing, eating, and moving around?
- What equipment might she need at home, and how is that arranged and funded?
- Will she need ongoing rehabilitation such as physio, speech therapy, or occupational therapy, and where does that happen?
- Is a home visit to check the house is safe something that can be arranged before she leaves?


Medications and safety at home

- Can someone go through all her current medications with me and explain what each one is for?
- How will her medications be managed if she has difficulty doing that herself?
- What warning signs should I watch for that mean I need to get help urgently?
- What do I do and who do I call if something concerns me after she is home?


Follow-up and who to contact

- Who is my main point of contact from here until discharge?
- How do I reach the team if I have questions before she leaves hospital?
- What follow-up appointments or reviews will she have after discharge, and will I be told about them?
- Is there a support service or resource for family members in my situation that you can point me to?
