# Diagnosis — insurer claim call script

Panel: `diagnosis-insurance-call`  ·  Country: NZ

---

What to say:

Hi, my name is [your full name] and I hold an income protection policy with Partners Life. I'd like to speak with someone who can help me understand whether I can make a claim under my policy, and if so, how to go about it. I have recently received a medical diagnosis and I want to check what cover I may be entitled to.

What to ask:

- Does my income protection policy cover my current situation, and what are the specific conditions that need to be met for me to make a claim?
- Does this policy pay out on diagnosis alone, or only once I am unable to work?
- Is there a stand-down or waiting period before payments begin, and how long is the benefit period?
- What is the monthly benefit amount I would receive if I qualify?
- Are there any exclusions or conditions in my policy that might affect my eligibility?
- What do I need to submit to make a claim, for example medical certificates, financial records, or forms from my employer?
- Are there any time limits I need to be aware of for lodging a claim?
- I also believe I may hold a trauma or critical illness policy with Partners Life. Can you check whether that is the case and whether it might also be relevant here?
- Can you please confirm all of this in writing, including the next steps and any forms I need to complete?

What to listen for:

- Whether the policy pays from diagnosis or only from when you are unable to work, as these are very different triggers and both are worth knowing about
- The exact waiting period before payments start, which with Partners Life income protection policies is often 4, 8, or 13 weeks, so you know when money would actually arrive
- How long the benefit period lasts, for example two years, five years, or to age 65
- Any exclusions that relate to pre-existing conditions or the specific nature of your diagnosis
- Whether they flag any other policy on your file that could also lead to a claim, including trauma cover
- Any deadlines for lodging a claim, so you do not miss a window
- A commitment to send you the policy wording and a summary of next steps in writing before you do anything else, especially before you make any changes to the policy
