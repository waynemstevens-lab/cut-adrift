# Diagnosis — questions for the appointment

Panel: `diagnosis-gp-questions`  ·  Country: NZ

---

Understanding what you've been told

- Can you explain my diagnosis in plain language, without medical jargon?
- Can you write down the diagnosis and the plan so I have it to refer to at home?
- What is happening in my body, and what does that mean for me day to day?
- What is the likely timeline from here, and what are the key stages I should know about?
- What happens if I decide not to start any treatment right now?


The plan and my options

- What are all the treatment options available to me?
- Can you walk me through what each option involves, step by step?
- What are the side effects of each option, including ones that might not be obvious?
- Are there side effects that I should contact someone about immediately if they happen?
- Is there anything I can do to reduce or manage the side effects?
- Am I able to get a second opinion, and how would I go about arranging that?
- Is my treatment available publicly through Te Whatu Ora, or would any of my options be private or funded differently?


Tests, results, and records

- Are there any further tests I need before treatment can start, and what are they for?
- How and when will I get the results of any tests?
- Can I have copies of my results, letters, and scan reports for my own records?
- What would the test results change about the plan?


Practical and work impact

- How long am I likely to need off work, and will that be all at once or in shorter periods?
- Will I be well enough to work during treatment, or is that unlikely?
- Should I contact Work and Income or ACC, or is there any financial support I should know about?
- Are there support services you would recommend, such as a social worker, counsellor, or Cancer Society?
- Is there anything I should avoid or change in my daily life right now?


Follow-up and staying in contact

- Who is my main point of contact from here, and how do I reach them?
- If something worries me before my next appointment, who should I call?
- When is my next appointment or review, and how will that be arranged?
- Is there a nurse specialist or patient navigator I can speak to if I have questions between appointments?
