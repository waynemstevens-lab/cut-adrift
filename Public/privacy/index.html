Privacy

What we do with your information

Cut Adrift is a free tool. We don't have accounts, we don't send emails, and we don't sell data. Here's exactly what happens when you use this site.

What you type stays in your browser

Your answers to the intake questions are stored only in your browser's session storage — a temporary space that is cleared automatically when you close the tab. Nothing you enter is saved to a database or linked to your identity.

Your generated plan is also stored in session storage so you can scroll back through it without reloading. It disappears when the tab closes.

Your answers are sent to an AI to generate your plan

When you reach the plan page, your intake answers are sent to our AI backend, which uses them to generate a personalised plan. The AI is provided by Anthropic. Your answers are processed to generate a response and are not used to train AI models.

Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that processing: anthropic.com/privacy.

We temporarily record your IP address for rate limiting

To prevent abuse and keep the tool free and available for everyone, we limit the number of plans that can be generated from a single IP address per day. Your IP address is stored temporarily for this purpose and deleted automatically after 24 hours. It is not linked to your answers or your plan.

No cookies. No tracking. No analytics.

Cut Adrift does not use cookies. We do not run advertising trackers, social media pixels, or analytics scripts. We do not know who you are, where you came from, or what you searched for to find us.

Infrastructure

This site is hosted on Cloudflare. Network-level data — such as IP addresses and request metadata — may be processed by Cloudflare in the normal course of serving web traffic. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Questions

If you have questions about how this site handles data, you can reach us at privacy@cutadrift.org.
