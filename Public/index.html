Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to notify ANZ Bank New Zealand of the death of my father, Robert James Whitford, who passed away on [date of death]. I am his son and the executor named in his will, and I am writing in that capacity as I begin to administer his estate.

I do not have a specific account number to hand, and would be grateful if you could locate any accounts held in his name from the details provided. …plus the documents they'll ask for, and a line asking them to stop all mail in his name — ready to sign and send.
